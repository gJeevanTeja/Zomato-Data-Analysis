# Zomato-Data-Analysis
Conducted end-to-end restaurant data analysis using Python, SQL, and Power BI to analyze restaurant ratings, votes, pricing, online ordering, and booking trends.
• Performed EDA in Python, cleaned and transformed data, stored datasets in SQL, and developed interactive Power BI dashboards for business insights.
• Identified key insights including Dining as the most popular restaurant category, higher vote concentration around 4.0–4.4 ratings, and low table-booking adoption compared to online ordering.
• Analyzed restaurant types, ratings, customer voting patterns, and cost distribution to understand consumer preferences and restaurant performance.
